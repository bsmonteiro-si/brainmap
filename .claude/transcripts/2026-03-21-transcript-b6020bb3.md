## Assistant



The fix is straightforward: wrap the edit-mode `<textarea>` in the same centering container div used for view mode, and auto-size the textarea to fit its content so the container can center it properly.

**Root cause:** The view-mode `<div class="canvas-text-node-body">` uses flexbox centering (`align-items: center; justify-content: center`), but the edit-mode `<textarea>` has `flex: 1` which stretches it to fill the entire card, and textarea content always starts at the top.

**Fix:** Reuse the `.canvas-text-node-body` wrapper around the textarea + auto-height the textarea to its content, so the wrapper centers the textarea itself.
